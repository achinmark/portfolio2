Federal Communications Commission Redesign

  • The Format: Mobile & Desktop
  • The Challenge: The current FCC website was not easily understood due to unconnected pages, broken links, and a chaotic layout.
  • The Details: Our team conducted a Heuristic Evaluation, Redlining, Matrix (Pain Points and Potential Solutions), User Tests (Round 1 - Initial, Round 2 - Interaction, Round 3 - Responsive A/B), Sketching, Cardsorting, Navigation Strategy, and Site Mapping.
  • The Result: The redesigned FCC website and mobile apps helped users spend less time searching and more time acting through clearer formatting and more accessible terminology.
