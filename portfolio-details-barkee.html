Barkee Dog Training App

  • The Format: Mobile
  • The Challenge: During quarantine, dog owners struggled to find time and resources with which to train their pets with.
  • The Details: Our team conducted a Competitor Analysis (4 apps), Prioritization Matrix, User Flow, Paper to Digital, and User Tests (3 tests, 5 tasks) specifically geared towards dog owners.
  • The Result: With the Barkee App, dog owners can enroll in online training, receive personalized feedback, connect with other owners, and order pet supplies with a couple of simple taps.
