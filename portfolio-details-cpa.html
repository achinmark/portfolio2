Children’s Performing Arts Redesign

  • The Format: Website
  • The Challenge: The Children’s Performing Arts website failed to direct parents to the parent portal and class sign-up page.
  • The Details: Our group conducted a Heuristic Evaluation, User Interviews, Prioritization Survey, Pain Point Categorization (to divide and conquer the changes that needed to be made in Display, Wording, User Action), and multiple Style Guides that were reviewed by parents of young children.
  • The Result: The redesigned website and mobile app enabled parents to quickly locate the parent portal and successfully enroll their children in classes.
