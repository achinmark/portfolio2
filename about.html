ABOUT ME

I'm a Marketing Associate/Dancer with the James Sewell Ballet and an aspiring UX/UI Designer.

My passion for design was born out of my desire to help others communicate in universal ways that transcend language. Much like the performing arts, the process and the results of a design project rely on both the artist’s commitment to the process, as well as, the audience’s reception. I believe the most beautiful aspect of design is when a concept resonates with people so deeply that it alters their life perspective for the better.


Here are some of my recent marketing, design, and dance endeavors:

  • 2021-2020 — UX/UI Design Certificate, The University of Minnesota (St. Paul, MN)
  • Present-2018 — Marketing Associate, James Sewell Ballet (Minneapolis, MN)
  • Present-2019 — Assistant To The Director, The University of Utah (Incheon, Korea)
  • Present-2018 — Guest Lecturer, The University of Utah (Salt Lake City, UT)
  • 2019 — Performer, Cannes Dance Festival (Cannes, France)
  • 2017 — Intern, Breast Cancer Action (San Francisco, CA)
  • 2016 — Project Developer, National Alliance on Mental Illness (Salt Lake City, UT)
  • 2016-2015 — Instructor, Ballet West Academy (Salt Lake City, UT)
