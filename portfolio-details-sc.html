Soundcloud Redesign

  • The Format: Mobile & Website
  • The Challenge: Soundcloud is an online audio distribution platform and music sharing website where artists can showcase their work. However, the current desktop and mobile apps are not supportive in helping artists develop their own brand or encouraging to those who desire music collaboration.
  • The Details: Our team conducted Interface Analysis, User Interviews and Surveys (with active Soundcloud Artists), Ideation, Definition, User Flow Charts/Maps/Matrices, and User Tests to formulate Adobe XD Prototypes and a Bootstrap 4 Website on GitHub.
  • The Result: By redesigning artists’ bio pages to look more presentable and personable, we are adding a valuable networking asset to the Soundcloud brand. When artists visit each other’s bio pages, they will feel more informed and inspired to reach out to collaborate with one another, whether on the mobile or desktop app.
